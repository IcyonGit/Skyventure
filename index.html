About SkyVenture Aviation

Your trusted partner in aviation at Brooks Field Airport

We are dedicated to providing safe, professional flight training and aircraft rental services to the Marshall, Michigan aviation community. Our mission is to help aspiring pilots achieve their dreams with the highest standards of safety and excellence.

Safety First

Our aircraft are meticulously maintained with regular inspections and modern avionics to ensure your safety in the air.

Professional Excellence

Community Focus

We're proud to serve the Marshall aviation community, fostering a love of flight and supporting local aviation education.
